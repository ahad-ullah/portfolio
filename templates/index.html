Hello, welcome to

Star Therapy

Star Therapy Services is a family-oriented health care clinic located in the city of Miami Florida. Since 2016 we have been dedicated in helping children and their families living with Speech-Language developmental delays and disorders. Inspired by the love for kids, our mission is to render the best quality of care and bring light to all whose life we touch.

My Services

What I Do?

Speach Therapy

Speech therapy is the assessment and treatment of communication problems and speech disorders.

Sensory Integration

Sensory integration is a term that gets thrown around a lot by teachers and therapists and staff members that work with young people that have autism because it’s so vital to their growth.

Early Intervention

Early intervention is the term used to describe the services and supports that are available to babies and young children with developmental delays and disabilities and their families.

Parent Coaching

Parent coaching is a completely judgment-free, confidential support to help you parent at your best - even when things are at their worst.
